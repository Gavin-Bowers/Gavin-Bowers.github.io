Space Escape VR

About the Project

This was my last project at Evergreen. The project was the focus of a class called Software Engineering. It was meant to emulate a production pipeline at a company, and was structured around sprints. I led the team, which had eight members. The timeline was six weeks, with two weeks of initial design and setup, and two two-week sprints to work on features. The project is a VR escape room game with a sci-fi setting. The coding was done with UE5 blueprints. I did most of the coding, since my teammates struggled to learn the engine. The project featured some original 3D models and textures created by my teammates.

Technologies Used

  • Unreal Engine 5
  • Blueprints
  • 3D Modeling
  • VR Development
  • Meta Quest Development
